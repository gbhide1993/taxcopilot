1. INTRODUCTION

This Civil Appeal is filed under Section 96 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 against the judgment and decree dated January 10, 2024 passed by the District Court in Civil Suit No. 456/2023. The Appellant respectfully submits this appeal challenging the findings of the learned trial court on both questions of law and fact.

2. FACTS SUMMARY

The Appellant entered into a Sale Agreement dated March 1, 2023 with the Respondent for the purchase of property bearing No. 45, Green Park Extension. The Appellant paid an advance of Rs. 50,00,000/- as earnest money. The Respondent failed to execute the sale deed within the stipulated time period of 90 days despite repeated requests and notices. The trial court erroneously dismissed the suit for specific performance filed by the Appellant.

3. LEGAL POSITION

The Appellant submits that the impugned judgment is contrary to the settled legal position on the following grounds:

3.1 Specific Performance Act, 1963

Section 10 of the Specific Performance Act, 1963 clearly provides for specific performance of contracts. The trial court failed to appreciate that all conditions precedent were fulfilled by the Appellant.

3.2 Contract Act, 1872

Section 73 of the Indian Contract Act provides for compensation for breach of contract. The Respondent's failure to perform constitutes a clear breach entitling the Appellant to relief.

4. PRAYER

In light of the above submissions, it is most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may be pleased to:

  • Set aside the impugned judgment and decree dated January 10, 2024
  • Grant decree for specific performance of the Sale Agreement dated March 1, 2023
  • Award costs of this appeal to the Appellant
  • Pass any other order as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit in the interest of justice
